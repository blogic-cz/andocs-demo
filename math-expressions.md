# Math Expressions

Andocs supports LaTeX math rendering via KaTeX. Use `$$` delimiters for math blocks.

> **Note:** Inline math with single `$` is not enabled. Use `$$` blocks for all formulas.

## Quadratic Formula

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## Gaussian Integral

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

## Matrix

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\
0 & \sin\theta & \cos\theta
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Sum and Product

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
$$

$$
\prod_{i=1}^{n} x_i = x_1 \cdot x_2 \cdots x_n
$$

## Standard Deviation

$$
\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} (x_i - \mu)^2}
$$

## Einstein's Mass-Energy Equivalence

$$
E = mc^2
$$
